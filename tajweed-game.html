How to Play

Game Instructions

  1. Select your difficulty level (Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced)
  2. Read the Quranic text displayed on the screen
  3. A word will be highlighted in the text
  4. Identify the Tajweed rule applied in the highlighted word
  5. Select the correct answer from the options provided
  6. Get immediate feedback on your answer
  7. Continue to the next question
  8. Complete all 10 questions to see your final score
