Basic Tajweed Course

Tajweed is the set of rules governing the way in which the words of the Quran should be pronounced during recitation. Learning Tajweed is essential for every Muslim as it helps in reciting the Quran correctly as it was revealed to Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).

Our Basic Tajweed course is designed to teach students the fundamental rules of Tajweed and help them apply these rules while reciting the Quran. The course is suitable for beginners as well as those who have some knowledge of Quran recitation but want to improve their Tajweed.

What You Will Learn:

  • Correct pronunciation of Arabic letters
  • Rules of Noon Sakinah and Tanween
  • Rules of Meem Sakinah
  • Rules of Laam Sakinah
  • Rules of Madd (prolongation)
  • Rules of stopping (Waqf) and starting (Ibtida)
  • Common mistakes in recitation and how to avoid them

Teaching Methodology:

Our teachers use a combination of theoretical explanations and practical exercises to ensure that students not only understand the rules but can also apply them correctly. Each student receives personalized attention and regular feedback on their recitation.

The course includes recitation practice of selected Surahs from the Quran, with a focus on applying the Tajweed rules learned during the classes.

Hifz ul Quran Course

Memorizing the Holy Quran (Hifz) is one of the most virtuous acts in Islam. Allah (SWT) has made the Quran easy to remember and promised great rewards for those who memorize it. Our Hifz ul Quran course is designed to help students memorize the entire Quran with proper Tajweed under the guidance of experienced Huffaz teachers.

This course requires dedication and consistency from the student. Our teachers work closely with each student to develop a personalized memorization plan based on their capacity and schedule.

What You Will Learn:

  • Memorization techniques for efficient learning
  • Proper pronunciation and Tajweed rules
  • Revision strategies to maintain memorization
  • Understanding the meaning of what is memorized
  • Connection between different parts of the Quran

Teaching Methodology:

Our Hifz program follows a structured approach that includes daily new memorization (Sabaq), revision of recently memorized portions (Sabqi), and regular review of previously memorized parts (Manzil).

Each student is assigned a dedicated teacher who monitors their progress, provides guidance, and ensures that the memorization is done with proper Tajweed. The teacher also helps the student develop a strong connection with the Quran by explaining the meanings and context of the verses being memorized.

Parents are encouraged to be involved in the process, especially for younger students, to provide support and encouragement at home.

Phase 1: Foundation

  • Memorization of Short Surahs (Juz Amma)
    4-8 weeks
  • Development of Memorization Techniques
    Ongoing

Phase 2: Progressive Memorization

  • Systematic Memorization of Remaining Juz
    Ongoing
  • Regular Revision Schedule
    Daily
  • Periodic Assessments
    Monthly

Phase 3: Consolidation and Certification

  • Complete Revision of the Quran
    3-6 months
  • Final Assessment

Namaz & Kalmas Course

Prayer (Salah/Namaz) is one of the five pillars of Islam and is obligatory for every Muslim. Our Namaz & Kalmas course is designed to teach the fundamentals of Islamic prayers, including the correct method of performing Salah and memorizing the six Kalmas (declarations of faith).

This course is ideal for beginners, new Muslims, and children who are learning to pray. It provides a solid foundation in the essential practices of Islam.

What You Will Learn:

  • The six Kalmas with proper pronunciation and meaning
  • Wudu (ablution) - steps and requirements
  • Correct postures and movements in Salah
  • Recitations required in Salah with proper pronunciation
  • Different types of prayers (Fard, Sunnah, Nafl)
  • Special prayers (Jumu'ah, Eid, Janazah)
  • Common mistakes in prayers and how to avoid them

Teaching Methodology:

Our teachers use visual demonstrations, step-by-step instructions, and interactive practice sessions to ensure that students learn the correct method of performing Salah. The course includes memorization of the required recitations with proper pronunciation and understanding of their meanings.

For children, we use engaging teaching methods including stories, activities, and visual aids to make learning enjoyable and effective.

Arabic Language Course

Arabic is the language of the Quran and holds a special place in Islamic studies. Learning Arabic helps Muslims understand the Quran, Hadith, and other Islamic texts in their original language, leading to a deeper connection with their faith.

Our Arabic Language course is designed to teach Modern Standard Arabic (MSA) with a focus on Quranic Arabic. The course caters to students of all levels, from complete beginners to those looking to advance their existing knowledge.

What You Will Learn:

  • Arabic alphabet and pronunciation
  • Reading and writing Arabic script
  • Basic vocabulary and common expressions
  • Grammar rules and sentence structure
  • Conversation skills for everyday situations
  • Quranic vocabulary and expressions
  • Translation skills

Teaching Methodology:

Our Arabic language program uses a communicative approach that emphasizes practical usage of the language. Students learn through interactive exercises, conversation practice, reading texts, and writing assignments.

For Quranic Arabic, we focus on vocabulary and grammatical structures commonly found in the Quran, helping students understand the divine text better.

The course is divided into levels, allowing students to progress at their own pace and build a solid foundation before moving to more advanced concepts.

Tafseer Course

Tafseer is the Arabic word for exegesis or interpretation of the Holy Quran. It involves understanding the meanings, context, and implications of Quranic verses. Our Tafseer course aims to provide students with a deeper understanding of the Quran through systematic study of its interpretation.

This course is suitable for intermediate to advanced students who have a basic understanding of the Quran and are looking to deepen their knowledge and connection with the divine text.

What You Will Learn:

  • Principles and methodologies of Tafseer
  • Historical context of Quranic revelations
  • Detailed explanation of selected Surahs and verses
  • Linguistic analysis of Quranic text
  • Thematic study of the Quran
  • Application of Quranic teachings in contemporary life

Teaching Methodology:

Our Tafseer course follows a structured approach that combines traditional and contemporary methods of Quranic interpretation. Students learn about the historical context of revelations, linguistic analysis, and the wisdom behind divine guidance.

The course includes study of classical Tafseer works as well as contemporary interpretations, providing students with a comprehensive understanding of how scholars throughout history have approached the Quran.

Interactive discussions, reflections, and assignments help students develop their own understanding and connection with the Quran while remaining grounded in authentic scholarship.
