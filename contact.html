Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about contacting us

How quickly will I receive a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent matters, we recommend contacting us via WhatsApp for faster assistance.

Can I schedule a free trial class through the contact form?

Yes, you can request a free trial class through our contact form. Simply select "Free Trial" as the subject and mention your preferred course and time slots in the message. Alternatively, you can use the dedicated registration form on our Register page.

How can I contact a specific teacher?

If you wish to contact a specific teacher, please mention their name in your message when using our contact form. Our administration will forward your message to the teacher, who will then get in touch with you directly.

I'm in a different time zone. When can I contact you?

We understand that our students come from different parts of the world. Our support team is available 24/7 to accommodate all time zones. You can contact us at any time that is convenient for you, and we'll respond as soon as possible.

How can I provide feedback about my learning experience?

We value your feedback! You can share your experience through our contact form by selecting "Feedback" as the subject. Additionally, you can visit our Testimonials page to submit a formal testimonial that may be featured on our website with your permission.

I'm interested in becoming a teacher. How can I apply?

If you're interested in joining our teaching team, please use our contact form and select "Other" as the subject. In your message, mention that you're applying for a teaching position and provide details about your qualifications and experience. Our HR team will review your application and get back to you.
