Meet Our Qualified Instructors

Learn from experienced and certified Quran teachers

At Islam Online Academy, we take pride in our team of highly qualified and dedicated teachers. All our instructors are certified in their respective fields and have extensive experience in teaching Quran, Tajweed, and Islamic studies.

Our teachers are not only knowledgeable but also passionate about sharing Islamic education with students worldwide. They are fluent in English, making it easier for international students to understand and learn effectively.

We offer both male and female teachers to accommodate the preferences of all students and families. Each teacher undergoes a rigorous selection process to ensure they meet our high standards of knowledge, teaching ability, and character.

Our Teacher Selection Process

How we ensure the highest quality of instruction

1

Qualification Verification

We verify all educational qualifications, certifications, and Ijazahs to ensure our teachers have the necessary knowledge and credentials.

2

Knowledge Assessment

Candidates undergo rigorous testing of their Quranic knowledge, Tajweed skills, and understanding of Islamic sciences relevant to their teaching area.

3

Teaching Demonstration

Candidates must demonstrate their teaching abilities through sample lessons, showing their communication skills and teaching methodology.

4

Character Evaluation

We assess the character and conduct of potential teachers to ensure they embody Islamic values and can be positive role models for students.

5

Training Program

Selected teachers undergo training in online teaching methodologies, student engagement, and our academy's curriculum and standards.

6

Continuous Evaluation

Our teachers receive regular feedback and evaluations to ensure they maintain high standards of teaching and continue to develop professionally.
