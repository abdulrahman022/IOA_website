Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our academy

How do online Quran classes work?

Our online Quran classes are conducted through video conferencing platforms like Zoom or Skype. Each student gets one-on-one attention from a qualified teacher. The teacher and student can see and hear each other, share screens, and use digital Quran resources for effective learning.

What are the fees for the courses?

Our course fees vary depending on the program and frequency of classes. We offer competitive rates starting from $40-$80 per month for regular classes. We also provide family discounts and flexible payment options. Please contact us for detailed fee information for your specific requirements.

What age groups do you teach?

We welcome students of all ages, from young children (as young as 4 years old) to adults. Our teaching methods are adapted according to the age and learning capacity of each student to ensure effective learning.

How long does it take to complete a course?

The duration varies depending on the course and the student's dedication. Basic Tajweed can take 3-6 months, while complete Quran memorization (Hifz) is a long-term commitment that can take several years. We create personalized learning plans based on individual goals and pace.

Do you provide learning materials?

Yes, we provide digital learning materials including Quran texts, Tajweed guides, and practice worksheets. For some courses, we may recommend physical books that can be purchased separately. Our teachers also create customized materials based on each student's needs.

What if I miss a class?

We understand that sometimes students may need to miss classes. If you inform us at least 6 hours in advance, we can reschedule the class. Each student is allowed up to 2 rescheduled classes per month, subject to teacher availability.
